What is Knoc Knoc? A: Founded in 2022, Knoc Knoc is a super app that combines curated and service oriented / caring cat providers to manage and provide a myriad of services to women of the 21st century. From wedding services, essential services, indulgence services, mummy and kiddy services, car workshop services, charity and volunteer services and to even ride hailing services, Knoc Knoc prides and aim to be the first and only super app that a women needs, at all times of the day and always. Meowww

Knoc Knoc works with more than 600 cat providers from the whole series of curated services to bring the best and affordable caring and pampering to women. With the whole suite of payment options, buy now pay later and installment payment plan, this is to allow women to have the option to choose on their favourite mode of payment and earn cashback and rebates.
